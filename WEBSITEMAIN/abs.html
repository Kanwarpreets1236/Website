Leg Raises

  1. Leg raises are a great exercise that targets your lower abs and helps strengthen the ligaments between your hips and abs creating the 'V-Cut' effect.
  2. An alternative for those more advanced is the hanging leg raise where you hang from a pull up bar and raise your legs in a similar fashion.
  3. Do 3-4 sets of 8-25+ depending on your level of fitness.

Side Jack-Knife.

  1. An alternative to the regular jack-knife, the side jack-knife targets the obliques directly.
  2. This exercise can be tricky to get right but remember to use your supporting leg to stabilize yourself.
  3. Focus on the contraction and remember to squeeze your obliques and not your lats.

Crunches

  1. Unlike the sit-up, crunches do not negatively impact the spine and can be performed safely by almost anyone.
  2. These target your abdominals directly with an emphasis on the upper region.

Plank

  1. The plank targets your core muscles isometrically creating a good balance with the other exercises in this workout.
  2. For those who find this too challenging you can drop down to your knees instead of your feet.

Flutter Kicks

  1. Sitting in a crunch position also allows you to activate your upper abs while doing this exercise.
  2. Flutter kicks are a great way to finish off your ab workout by burning out your core.
