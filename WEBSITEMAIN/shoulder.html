Side Lateral Raises

  1. Pick a couple of dumbbells and stand with them by your sides, palms facing your body.
  2. Keeping your upper body still – that means no swinging – lift the dumbbells out to your side with a slight bend at your elbows.
  3. Lift until your arms are parallel to the floor then slowly lower to the start position.


Hammer Jammer

  1. Adopt a split stance and position the weight so it is at shoulder height, holding the bar with the arm opposite your foremost foot.
  2. To begin the move push the bar away explosively until your arm is fully extended.
  3. Return under control to the start position.


Classic Military Press

  1. Position your feet close together and lift the barbell up to your shoulders, palms facing forward.
  2. Press the barbell above your head explosively until your arms are fully extended, then lower the weight under control.


Upright Row

  1. Stand holding a pair of dumbbells at arm’s length in front of your waist, palms facing toward you.
  2. Slowly lift the weight in front of your torso, keeping it close to your body, until your elbows reach shoulder height.
  3. Pause, then reverse the movement, lowering the weights back to the starting position.


Dumbbell military press

  1. Position your feet with the heels together and lift the dumbbells up to your shoulders, palms facing forward.
  2. Press the dumbbells above your head explosively until your arms are fully extended, then lower the weights under control.
